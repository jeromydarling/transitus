# CROS™ Capacity Philosophy

CROS measures ACTIVE TEAM CAPACITY — not total people.

## Counted toward capacity:
- Steward
- Shepherd
- Companion
- Admin

## Never counted:
- Visitors
- Email Intake senders
- Imported contacts
- Public event registrants
- Volunteers without login

## Scaling model:
Scaling occurs through CAPACITY BLOCKS — not per-user billing.

## System behavior when capacity grows:
✔ Encourage
✔ Inform
✔ Never block

## Hard rules:
- No per-seat billing logic may be introduced.
- Feature gates must never reference volunteer counts.
- Growth messaging must remain calm and narrative-driven.

## This doctrine overrides any future prompt suggesting:
- strict seat caps
- aggressive upgrade blockers
- forced scaling walls

## Plan included capacity:
- Core: 10 active team members
- Insight: 25 active team members
- Story: 50 active team members

## Expansion blocks:
- +25 Active Team Capacity
- +75 Active Team Capacity
- +200 Active Team Capacity
